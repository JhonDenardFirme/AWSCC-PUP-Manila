WHO ARE WE?

AWS Cloud Club - PUP Manila is a distinctive and university-wide organization, focusing on the impact of cloud technology to the community, based at the Polytechnic University of the Philippines (PUP).

MISSION

To foster a community of passionate learners leveraging AWS, and creating opportunities for students to give back and effect meaningful change

VISION

To foster a community of passionate learners leveraging AWS, and creating opportunities for students to give back and effect meaningful change

MEET OUR CLUB MASCOTS

Alfina, championing women and LGBTQIA+ individuals in tech

Alf, our guiding “AWStronaut” for all PUP students exploring AWS

Alfred, inspiring and supporting tech startup founders

Together, they infuse vibrant energy into our community, embodying the core mission and vision of AWS Cloud Club PUP.

CLUB DEPARTMENTS

CLOUD COMPUTING

Providing a deep dive into cloud technologies, students learn to architect, deploy, and manage cloud infrastructure at scale. Hands-on learning with AWS services covers compute, storage, networking, and serverless technologies.

DATA TECHNOLOGIES

Focusing on data engineering, analytics, and cloud-based data management, students gain hands-on experience in designing and optimizing data pipelines, leveraging cloud-native storage solutions, and performing large-scale data processing.

INTERNET OF THINGS

Exploring the intersection of cloud computing and connected devices, students learn to develop and manage IoT solutions within AWS ecosystems. Topics include secure and efficient device communication, real-time data collection and processing, and scalable IoT architectures.

SECURITY & NETWORKING

Focusing on securing cloud environments and optimizing network performance, students gain hands-on experience in designing robust security architectures, implementing identity and access management (IAM), and applying encryption strategies to protect sensitive data.

WEB DEVELOPMENT

Empowering students to design, develop, and deploy high-performance web applications using AWS technologies, the curriculum emphasizes scalable, cloud-native applications, modern web frameworks, serverless computing, and secure authentication.
